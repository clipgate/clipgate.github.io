How It Works

Three steps. Zero setup. Your way.

Install the extension and let it run automatically — or turn off auto-capture and save only what matters via right-click or Ctrl+Shift+S.

1

You Copy

Copy anything on any webpage. In auto mode, it's saved instantly. In selective mode, right-click → Save to ClipGate or press Ctrl+Shift+S.

2

We Classify

In under 5ms, ClipGate identifies what you copied — error trace, JSON, secret, command, URL — using pattern matching and entropy analysis.

3

We Protect

Secrets are detected, encrypted with AES-256-GCM, masked in the UI, and auto-expired. Everything stays on your machine. Nothing leaves.

Features

Not another clipboard history.
A smarter clipboard for everyone who works with text.

13-Type Classifier

Automatically identifies what you copied — URLs, errors, JSON, commands, paths, SQL, secrets, and more. Every copy is tagged the moment it happens.

Smart Secret Masking

Detects 18 secret patterns (AWS, GitHub, Stripe, OpenAI...). Shows known prefix + masked body + last 4 chars — like your bank dashboard.

AES-256 Encryption

Secrets are encrypted at rest inside your browser. Not plaintext. Not accessible from outside. Encryption key stored separately.

Auto-Expiry

Secrets auto-purge on a configurable timer (default: 2 hours, max: 2 days). Set it once, forget it. Your secrets don't linger.

Search & Filter

Find any clip instantly. Filter by type, search by content, keyboard shortcuts. / to search, Esc to clear.

Two Capture Modes

Auto mode saves every copy silently. Selective mode lets you choose — right-click → Save to ClipGate, or Ctrl+Shift+S. Switch in settings anytime.

18 Secret Patterns

AWS, GitHub, GitLab, Slack, Stripe, OpenAI, Anthropic, npm, PyPI, SendGrid, Twilio, Heroku, private keys, generic credentials, and high-entropy strings via Shannon analysis.

AES-256-GCM at Rest

Every detected secret is encrypted before touching IndexedDB. The key is stored separately in chrome.storage. Not visible in DevTools, not readable in disk forensics.

Configurable Auto-Expiry

Default: 2 hours. Max: 2 days. Secrets are purged every 60 seconds and filtered from the UI even before purge runs. Your call, your timeline.

Smart Masking

Shows the recognizable prefix (sk_live_, AKIA, ghp_) plus masked middle plus last 4 characters. You know which key it is without seeing the full secret.
