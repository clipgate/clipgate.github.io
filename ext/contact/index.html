Common Questions

Is ClipGate really free?

Yes. ClipGate is free to use as a binary download from the official site.

Does ClipGate send my data anywhere?

No. Everything is stored locally in your browser. Zero network requests, zero tracking, zero telemetry. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I uninstall and delete all data?

Right-click the ClipGate icon in your browser toolbar and select "Remove from Chrome" (or your browser's equivalent). This removes the extension and all stored data completely.

Which browsers are supported?

ClipGate works on all Chromium-based browsers: Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera, and Arc. Firefox support is coming soon.
